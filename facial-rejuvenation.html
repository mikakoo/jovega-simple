Facial Rejuvenation

What is a Rejuvenating Facial?

It is a wonderful facial for both men and women. This gentle treatment uses precise rhythmic movements which allow facial muscles to relax and tension to fade away.

No creams or oils are used.

The Benefits

  • Feels blissful, and leaves skin soft and smooth
  • Promotes a glowing complexion
  • Evens out skin tone and helps to combat acne and blemishes
  • Helps to relive sinus congestion
  • Reduces puffiness around the eyes
  • Used after cosmetic surgery; reduces swelling and accelerates healing
  • Deeply relaxing, helps to de-stress and regenerate

Cost

A 60 minute session costs £65
