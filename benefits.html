Benefits

Why use colonic hydrotherapy?

Stress, poor diet and our modern lifestyle can all have an effect on the colon. The use of Colonic Hydrotherapy helps to:

  • Maintain good elimination
  • Restore and strengthen peristalsis
  • Promote the growth of healthy gut bacteria

No special measures are required prior to a colonic, although it is highly beneficial to take a good quality probiotic 2 days prior to treatment.

I recommend BioNutri's EcoDophilus capsules which can be obtained directly from The Natural Dispensary.

Return to work/normal activities can resume following treatment.

Who can benefit?

  • Anyone wanting to achieve optimum health
  • People living sedentary lifestyles
  • Those embarking on a detoxification programme
  • Night shift workers
  • People who eat out regularly
  • Regular air-flight travellers

Some conditions that may be helped

  • Constipation
  • IBS
  • Diarrhoea
  • Flatulence
  • Gut disbiosis
  • Haemorrhoids
  • Diverticulosis
  • Bloating
  • Halitosis
  • Fatigue
  • Psoriasis
  • Eczema
  • Headaches
  • Candida

Debilitating health conditions of all kinds may benefit from the detoxifying effects of colon hydrotherapy because cleansing the colon can help to enable all systems of the body to function to optimum potential.
