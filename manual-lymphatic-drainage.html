Manual Lymphatic Drainage (MLD) - Vodder Technique

What is MLD?

MLD is an extremely gentle non-invasive therapy which promotes a healthy lymphatic system (the body's defence mechanism).

The lymphatic system helps to protect the body against infection, transports nutrients to cells, and clears metabolic waste, toxins and excess fluid from the body.

Very precise rhythmic movements are made over the skin; this stimulates lymph vessels allowing the flow of lymph to improve and for excess fluid, toxins and waste products to drain away. This treatment has an extremely calming effect on the nervous system which encourages deep relaxation.

MLD was developed in the 1930s by Dr Emil Vodder, and is now practised in hospitals and clinics across Europe.

The Benefits

  • Great to use alongside weight-loss programmes; (can speed up the removal of waste products and excessive fluid)
  • Reduces pain and swelling athletes especially boxers and other contact sports men and women find this treatment very useful
  • Cellulite; improves the texture of skin, helps to makes skin soft and smooth
  • Used before and after surgery MLD can help recovery rate as MLD helps to bring nourishment to cells and allows the body to relax and regenerate
  • A must for after cosmetic surgery and liposuction; reduces fibrosis and swelling
  • In pregnancy MLD helps to relive the body of fluid retention (swollen legs, arms and breasts)
  • Stress reduction and improved immunity
