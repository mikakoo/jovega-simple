Tips for good digestion

  • Chew well! Take time over meals.
  • Hydration is essential for health and wellbeing. Aim to drink 1.5-2 litres of fluid daily. Ideally this should be in the form of water, diluted fruit and vegetable juices and herb teas.
  • Take drinks at room temperature and spread throughout the day rather than consuming a large amount at once.
  • Avoid drinking large amounts of fluids with meals. Many people feel better with a little water with a meal, but try to limit this to a small glass.
  • Eat less processed and more whole food.
  • Eat smaller portions of high quality food.
  • Rotate your foods so that you have a varied diet.
  • Eat food that is in season, chemical free and locally grown where possible.
  • Avoid fizzy drinks and squash.
  • Minimise the use of tea and coffee.
  • Avoid chewing gum.
  • Be relaxed whilst eating – enjoy meals.
