Therapeutic Facial

This Therapeutic Facial uses various techniques including myofascial tissue release, lymphatic drainage, and aspects of Bowen and craniosacral therapy.

We use wonderful natural products which hydrate, nourish and accelerate skin cell renewal.

Every treatment is a personal experience tailored to the individual. Whilst the vitamin rich masks are working their magic, to enhance relaxation you may choose to have a hand, head or foot massage.

Revitalised skin gives a beautiful, natural glow at any age!

An hour of facial pampering using nature’s finest quality ingredients.
